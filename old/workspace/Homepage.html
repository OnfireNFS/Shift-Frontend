Appraisals

An appraisal is a document stating the approximate retail replacement value of an item. It is calculated using current market data, which reflects typical retail prices. The appraisal includes the cut, color, clarity, and either carat weight for diamonds or millimeter dimensions for gemstones, and the craftsmanship of the item. Your appraisal ensures that in the case of loss, theft, or damage your insurance company will give you an equal replacement of your item.

About Us

Diamond Gal started with a teenager in 1978 wanting to build her own business. Lana Hugh wanted to provide people with her favorite thing in the world, diamonds. Her saying was “dogs may be a guy’s best friend, but diamonds are everyone’s best friend.” She was determined to sell diamonds to as many people as she could. She started off with cheaper products and worked her way up to getting other jewelry companies to invest their product to her so she could sell them at her jewelry store. Her business was slow at first, but it quickly picked up pace and is now one of the top jewelry stores in the U.S.
